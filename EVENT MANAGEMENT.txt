                                       EVENT MANAGEMENT

INTRODUCTION
Event management is the process of planning, organizing, and executing events. It can include conferences, trade shows, weddings, and parties. Event management is a vital part of many organizational strategies. 

PURPOSE
The purpose of event management is to plan, organize, and execute events to achieve specific goals. Event management can help promote events, build brands, and create memorable experiences.

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENT
The functional requirements of event management include: understanding client needs, booking venues, scheduling speakers, managing attendee registration, coordinating logistics like transportation and accommodation, marketing the event, managing budgets, ensuring compliance with legal requirements, and delivering a successful event within time and financial constraints; essentially, all the tasks needed to plan and execute an event from start to finish, meeting the specific goals and objectives of the client.
